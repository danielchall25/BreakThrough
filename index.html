Description

The rough outline of the game concept is to structure it as a roguelike dungeon crawler. Our concept draws inspiration from the original Legend of Zelda games that were introduced during the 1980s. As far as controls, Downloader movement is intended for traditional PCs and uses the “WASD” keys for up, down, left and right. Player’s attack will be assigned to the left mouse button. Roguelike elements of the game are intended to be tied to the player’s story, ending with the player successfully escaping from the dungeon to the surface. While escaping the dungeon the player will encounter enemies, collect currency, and return to the starting point if the player dies. Upon death, the player will have the ability to purchase consumables or upgrades to help in their next attempt to escape the dungeon. These ideas are not set in stone and are liable to change as we discuss what the team wants for the end game.
